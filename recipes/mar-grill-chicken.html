Marinated Grilled Chicken Recipe

Home

Description

We will be cooking 4 delicious pieces of marinated grilled chicken using these main ingredients: Italian dressing, lemon pepper, and chicken cutlet.

This recipe will require 3 hours and 25 minutes of your time, including 5 minutes to prepare, 20 minutes to cook, and an additional 3 hours in between.

Ingredients

  • 4 chicken breast halves
  • 8 oz Italian dressing
  • 1/4 tsp lemon pepper
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. Rinse chicken and pat dry. Place in a shallow glass bowl and pour salad dressing over it. Cover and refrigerate for at least 3 hours.
  2. Lightly oil grill and preheat to medium high.
  3. Remove chicken breasts from marinade. Season with lemon pepper and salt to taste. Grill over medium high heat for 10 to 15 minutes on each side, or until chicken is cooked through and juices run clear.
